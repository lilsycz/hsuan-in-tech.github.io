1) Why this program

I want a structured, inclusive path into IT. My goal is to translate my architecture/game-env background into web and software skills, starting with practical programming, UX basics, and project work that prepares me for an entry-level tech role.

2) Why I will do well

I am disciplined, fast-learning, and detail-oriented. I have shipped complex studio projects, learn tools quickly, and document my process. I thrive in focused sprints, give/receive feedback well, and enjoy solving problems that help people.

3) Personal letter

Hello! I’m Hsuan, a designer moving into tech. My training in digital architecture taught me systems thinking, visual problem-solving, and clear communication. Over the past year I’ve been building small interactive demos and a simple portfolio site, and I discovered how much I enjoy writing clean, readable code and iterating with users in mind. In this program I hope to solidify my foundations (HTML/CSS/JS, Python, Git, UX), collaborate with peers, and graduate with a few small but complete apps I can maintain and improve. My longer-term aim is a junior front-end/creative-tech role where I can grow, contribute to an inclusive team, and keep learning.

4) Other information

I’m available for remote/onsite study, have a reliable laptop and stable internet, and can commit to deadlines. English is fluent; Swedish is in progress. I’m motivated to practice daily and support classmates.
